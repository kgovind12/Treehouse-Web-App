Memory Card Game

MEMORY GAME

Try matching the facts below as best you can, through memory!

Each card will have a mathematical equation, definition, or name of the formula (e.g. distance formula, fundamental theorem of calculus, etc.). Click on any random card, then pick another random card. If they match, awesome! If not, keep in mind where the two cards were. Continue clicking on more pairs to get matches, until you've reached all of them!
